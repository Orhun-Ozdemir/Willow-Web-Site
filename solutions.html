Frequently asked

Questions about our solutions.

Here are the answers to the most common questions about our IoT capabilities and deployments.

Which industries does WillowSoft serve with IoT solutions?

WillowSoft serves industrial automation, energy and solar plants, smart buildings, telecommunications, smart logistics and cold chain, healthcare, smart agriculture and greenhouses, and field telemetry for utilities and infrastructure.

Does WillowSoft provide complete IoT solutions or just devices?

Yes. WillowSoft delivers the entire stack — sensors and gateways, LoRaWAN connectivity, backend APIs, PostgreSQL storage, web and mobile dashboards, alerting workflows and admin panels.

What sensor types can WillowSoft deploy in industrial environments?

Ultrasonic level (WillowSonic), air quality (WillowAir), Modbus RTU bridges (WillowMod), GNSS trackers (WillowGPS), pressure (WillowPre), tilt (WillowTilt), soil moisture (WillowMos), wind speed (WillowAne), temperature and humidity (WillowTemp), CO₂ (WillowCO2) and door open/close (WillowSens).
