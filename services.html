Frequently asked

Questions before you brief us.

Engineering buyers usually arrive with the same operational questions. Here are the answers — the rest happens in the discovery call.

What engineering services does WillowSoft offer?

WillowSoft delivers end-to-end connected product engineering across eight disciplines: embedded hardware design (custom PCB, RF, low-power), firmware development (STM32, RTOS, OTA), backend APIs (REST + WebSocket, auth, ingestion), PostgreSQL database architecture, web platforms and admin panels, native iOS (Swift) + native Android (Kotlin) + Flutter cross-platform mobile apps, VR & simulation, and IoT cloud telemetry.

Does WillowSoft deliver complete connected products end-to-end?

Yes. WillowSoft owns every layer of a connected product — hardware, firmware, RF/LoRaWAN connectivity, backend, database, mobile and web interfaces — so customers do not need to coordinate multiple vendors or translate requirements between disconnected teams.

Which technologies does WillowSoft specialise in?

Core stacks include STM32WL Cortex-M4 microcontrollers, LoRaWAN 1.1.0, Modbus RTU, BLE, Wi-Fi, FreeRTOS, REST APIs, WebSocket, PostgreSQL, Swift (native iOS), Kotlin (native Android), Flutter, Three.js / WebGL for web, and Unity for VR simulations.

Can WillowSoft handle production-readiness and certification?

Yes. WillowSoft's engineering process includes RF tuning, device stability, certification support (CE, FCC, TELEC, KC), production handoff and ongoing field improvements.

Do you sign NDAs before sharing technical details?

Yes. WillowSoft signs mutual NDAs at the start of any commercial conversation involving customer hardware specifications, intellectual property, or sensitive deployment data.

What's a typical project timeline?

Hardware prototypes typically take 4–8 weeks. Full connected product systems (hardware + firmware + backend + dashboard + mobile) typically take 4–6 months from kickoff to field-ready deployment.

Who owns the IP at the end of the project?

The customer owns the project IP — schematics, firmware source, backend code, mobile apps. WillowSoft retains rights to its internal reference libraries, certified RF blocks and reusable platform components that pre-exist the engagement.
