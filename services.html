Solar Plant Remote Monitoring

Predictive maintenance layer for distributed solar fields.

The Problem: Manual inspections of inverters and transformers led to high operational overhead and late fault detection across thousands of distributed solar panels.

The Approach: WillowSoft designed and deployed IP67-rated Modbus bridges (WillowMod) collecting inverter registry data, transmitting via LoRaWAN to a high-concurrency API and PostgreSQL telemetry database.

"WillowSoft unified our field devices and dashboards into one reliable system. Our maintenance cycles are now purely predictive." — Lead Operations Engineer, SLB Energy

Cold Chain Logistics & Quality Control

Real-time asset tracking and environmental monitoring.

The Problem: Temperature fluctuations and open-door incidents during logistics transit caused product quality concerns, with high battery drain on legacy cellular trackers.

The Approach: WillowSoft designed a custom low-power GNSS tracker (WillowGPS) with integrated temperature and door open/close sensors, running custom low-power firmware combined with a native mobile dashboard for driver alerts.

"The hardware battery life and native dashboard alert responsiveness exceeded our expectations. An exceptional end-to-end telemetry system." — Logistics Director, FMCG Group
